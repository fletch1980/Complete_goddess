🧠 Mind-Muscle Cues

Hip Thrust
Drive through your heels. At the top, squeeze your glutes for 2 full seconds before lowering. Think "crush a walnut." Feel it in the glute, not your lower back.
Cable / Band Kickback
Slow the movement down. Squeeze at full extension for 1–2 sec. If you feel it in your hip flexor or back, you've gone too fast.
Romanian Deadlift
Feel the stretch in your hamstrings on the way down. Drive your hips forward to stand — glutes finish the movement, not your back.
Sumo Squat
Push knees out over toes. Pause at the bottom. On the way up, squeeze inner thighs and glutes together at the same time.
Every Exercise
If you don't feel the target muscle, drop the weight 20% and slow down. Mind-muscle connection beats heavy and sloppy every single time.
